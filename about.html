About Us

Studio Fixx is designed to be a community gym where everyone is welcome. All members have 24 hours unlimited access and it is conveniently located at 24-68, Studio Fixx, Block C, California, United States.

It is also a facility where student athletes from all sports, as well as students not participating in organized sports who just want to improve their overall fitness, can supplement their strength and conditioning training, without any limitations or concerns about hours of availability.
